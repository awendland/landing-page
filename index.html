Cloc

Android App

Cloc was created to ease the process of using a timer. Most phone timers are slower then the basic turn and go style of old-style analog timers. Cloc was made to fix this problem. Why should the whole process of setting a timer take almost as long as the time you want to countdown? Cloc was created to do this one function and to do it well.
